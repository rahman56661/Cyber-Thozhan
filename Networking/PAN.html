What is PAN?

Personal Area Network - Connecting Your Personal Devices

Definition

A Personal Area Network (PAN) is a network used for connecting personal devices for individual use.

Simple explanation: When you connect your own devices together for personal use, that's a PAN.

Examples

Phone to Phone File Sharing

Sharing files between two phones using Bluetooth technology.

How it works: Enable Bluetooth on both devices, pair them, and transfer files directly.

Phone to Earbuds/Music Devices

Connecting your phone to wireless audio devices like earbuds or speakers.

How it works: Pair your Bluetooth earbuds with your phone to listen to music or take calls wirelessly.

Mobile Hotspot

Using your phone's mobile data to create a WiFi network for other devices.

How it works: Enable mobile hotspot on your phone, then connect your laptop or other devices to this personal WiFi network.
